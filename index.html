Luca Ferretti's research page:
Precision Infectious Disease Epidemiology group

Luca is a group leader and Career Development Fellow of the Pandemic Sciences Institute, part of the Nuffield Department of Medicine of the University of Oxford.
See his institutional PSI and BDI webpages.

The group's research focuses on mathematical and statistical models, with special focus on infectious disease epidemiology and precision epidemiology.
We also develop models and analyses with wider applications to genomics, evolution, sociology and complex systems.

Our group is based at the Big Data Institute.
We work in close collaboration with several other groups at PSI, especially with the Fraser, Lythgoe, Panovska-Griffiths and Hay groups.
