Luca Ferretti's research page:
Precision Infectious Disease Epidemiology group

Luca is a Career Development Fellow of the Pandemic Sciences Institute, part of the Nuffield Department of Medicine of the University of Oxford. We are based at the Big Data Institute.

Our research focuses on mathematical and statistical models for infectious disease epidemiology. We are also interested in wider applications to genomics, evolution, sociology and complex systems.

We work in close collaboration with several other groups at PSI, especially with the Fraser, Lythgoe, Panowska-Griffits and Hay groups.

Research lines

  • Precision epidemiology for emerging pathogens
  • Digital pandemic preparedness
  • Vaccine trial modelling
  • HIV epidemiology and evolution
  •  
  • Evolutionary epidemiology
  • Pathogen genomics
  • Population genetics and evolution
  • Dynamics on complex networks
  • Structure of human interactions
